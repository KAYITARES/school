Welcome to Saint Joseph Website

Saint Joseph was established in 1997, with just 130 number of students. Today the school has grown to more than 1600 students with 56 different nationalities spanning from two years of age through Grade 12. Saint Joseph is centrally located on a lush 26 acre campus in Kicukiro, Kigali. Its facilities include a state of the art gymnasium, pool, music and band room, dining hall, boarding houses and soccer fields. Saint Joseph is the only school in Rwanda offering the International Baccalaureate (IB) Diploma Program, which helps our students get into competitive secondary globally. Saint Joseph is also the only school in Rwanda with Label France Education accreditation, helping students pursue further education and careers in Francophone countries. Our boarding facilities are offered from Grades 7 -12.

Saint Joseph offers two options for families from Nursery through Middle School. Parents can choose the English track, where students study all content areas in English, and then French and Kinyarwanda as additional languages. Alternatively, parents can choose our bilingual 50/50 program, where a portion of the day is studied in English (English, Science) and the other portion of the day is in French (Math, French, Social Studies). Our experienced Admissions Director can help parents choose the best path for their children. Once in High School, our students study for the Cambridge International Examinations IGCSEs in Grades 9 and 10, and the IB Diploma Program in Grades 11 and 12. Green Hills Academy’s experienced College and Career Counselor helps students prepare and apply for universities.There are many other great things about Green Hills Academy, such as our dedicated and knowledgeable staff, our expansive after school programs, our varieties of language choices and our boarding facilities. Please feel free to email us or make an appointment to meet our Admissions Director if you have questions.
