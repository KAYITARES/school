Welcome to Saint Joseph Primary Web site

Saint Joseph Primary Section consists of six classes, with two sections-one section takes English as the command language and the other takes French as the command language. Whatever the Section students go to, they take English as a compulsory subject for the French Section and French as a compulsory subject for the English Section. When pupils join Primary six, they are taught in English as a command language in a bid to prepare for their national exams that are set in English. These languages are taught by well trained teachers who ensure that they contribute towards building a competent breed of bilingual pupils. Knowing both languages enables us equip our students with linguistic skills.

Ecole primaire saint joseph pupils are taught both English and French, depending on the section one chooses. The Primary Section runs for six years from Primary one to Primary six. All the pupils are required to have knowledge in English language in a bid to prepare them for their national examination. All teachers are given the teaching materials and students are given all learning facilities they require for effective acquisition of knowledge and skills. To this end, we have a well-equipped computer laboratory and well stocked library to support the students in their academic journey.
